**Sales pipeline, revenue, and EBITDA measure WHAT you deliver. DORA metrics measure HOW you work.**
For example, we don't measure number of sales calls
**Why This Matters:**
- Gaming WHAT usually requires achieving the goal (can't fake revenue)
- Gaming HOW can bypass the goal entirely (can improve deployment frequency without shipping value)
The difference in incentives:
- When you optimize for a metric that measures WHAT, you are actually improving the end output / achieving the goal
- When you optimize for a metric that measures HOW, you are just managing the optics into the process (no impact, or even negative impact, on the WHAT i.e. the value delivered)
