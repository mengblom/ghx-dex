### Emergent Request Process — New

- New intake channel for urgent, unclassified issues (not clearly a defect or enhancement) arising during customer implementations or ERP migrations
- Previously handled ad hoc via Slack/email — unreliable and untracked
- Process: submit via existing ghxide.io portal under new “GHX Exchange Emergent Request” option

- 8–10 fields: issue type, description, GHX Advantage flag, product area, customer impact, severity (critical/major/minor), optional Jira/Salesforce ID
- Product team reviews submissions weekly; comments/status updates posted back in portal
- Piloted with one request via Microsoft Forms — worked; full rollout pending
- Alexis flagged anticipated pushback: “injecting red tape when I need an answer right now”

- Consistency across products (Exchange, e-invoicing) could be a key talking point to counter this
- Alexis keen to adopt a similar model for her team if rollout goes well
