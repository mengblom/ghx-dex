## Career Overview & Background

**Q: Tell me about your background and experience.**

Has been trying to instill data ownership mindset, moving from band-aid solutions to scalable architecture. Recently started moving disparate data sources into one reusable medallion architecture. Specializes in understanding both business needs and technical execution.
