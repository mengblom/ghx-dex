# Interview Scorecard: Principal Engineer - Data Architecture and Engineering

**Rating Scale:** Strong No | No | Mixed | Yes | Strong Yes

---

## Skills
- Deep hands-on experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL, SQL Server)
- Deep expertise in NoSQL databases (MongoDB, DynamoDB, DocumentDB)
- Database performance optimization at scale (query tuning, indexing, execution plans)
- Advanced data modeling and schema design (temporal models, event sourcing, complex domains)
- Application development skills (full-stack, backend, or data-intensive applications)
- Can review application code and architecture with focus on data access patterns
- Experience evaluating and selecting database technologies based on workload, cost, and usage patterns
- Cloud data platform expertise (AWS, Azure, or GCP) with cost optimization focus
- Data warehouse design and implementation (Snowflake, Redshift, etc.)
- ETL/ELT pipeline design and data processing architectures
- Event-driven architectures, change data capture, and streaming platforms (Kafka, Kinesis, Pub/Sub)
- Data architecture for ML/LLM applications (vector databases, embeddings, RAG patterns)
- Zero-downtime data migration and schema evolution strategies
- Data quality, monitoring, and observability practices
- Aligning data ownership models with domain boundaries
- Hands-on troubleshooting and optimization of production data systems
- Proficiency in modern programming languages (Python, Java, JavaScript/TypeScript, C#)
## Personality Traits
- Enabling mindset - succeeds by making others successful
- Educates and empowers rather than gatekeeps
- Hands-on and takes ownership
- Can influence without authority
- Pragmatic judgment - knows when to optimize for speed vs. perfection
- Comfortable with ambiguity and building from scratch
- Works effectively in modern agile/iterative development processes
- Collaborates effectively across teams (engineering, product, infrastructure, security)
- Communicates technical concepts effectively to executives and non-technical stakeholders
## Qualifications
- 10+ years of hands-on experience building software applications with heavy focus on data systems
- Strong application development background (not just database administration)
- Track record of establishing data standards and patterns across engineering organizations
- Experience leading data migrations and infrastructure modernization initiatives
- Experience with AI/LLM-assisted development tools and agentic software engineering practices
- Prior Principal Engineer, Staff Engineer, or senior technical leadership experience
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience
## Other/Details
- Healthcare or EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) domain knowledge
- Experience in enabling team or platform team models
- Experience with autonomous or cross-functional team environments
