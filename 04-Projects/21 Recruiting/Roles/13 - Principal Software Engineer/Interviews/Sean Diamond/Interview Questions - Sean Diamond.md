**Time Management:** This is a 30-minute screen, so keep answers focused. If Sean goes deep on one topic, you may need to skip or abbreviate others.

**Depth vs. Breadth:** For a Principal level screen, look for both breadth (have they done the required technologies?) and depth (can they discuss trade-offs and design decisions?).

**Sean-Specific Strengths to Explore:**
- 15+ years Java experience (exceeds 8+ requirement)
- JVM optimization expertise (30% CPU reduction)
- Multi-cloud experience (AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes)
- Polyglot persistence (MySQL, MongoDB, MSSQL, Oracle, Postgres, Redis)
- Search technology (MongoDB/Lucene at current role)
- Management experience transitioned to IC role
- MS in Computer Science from Georgia Tech

**Potential Areas to Probe:**
- Why transition from manager back to IC? Does he prefer hands-on work?
- ElasticSearch specifically (has Lucene but GHX needs ElasticSearch)
- Recent hands-on Java/Spring Boot work (current role is MongoDB/Lucene focused)
- Experience with offshore/vendor teams (resume doesn't explicitly mention)
- Event-driven architecture or rules engines (preferred but not on resume)

**Red Flags to Watch:**
- Rusty on Java/Spring Boot if focused on other technologies recently

**Green Flags:**
- Asks clarifying questions before answering
- Provides specific, concrete examples from real projects
- Discusses trade-offs and context in technical decisions
- Shows curiosity about GHX's technical challenges
- Demonstrates both technical depth and leadership capability
- Strong AWS/cloud experience (preferred) ✓
