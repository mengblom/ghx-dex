## Overview

Organization-wide initiative to adopt Jarvis AI platform and develop AI proficiency across engineering teams. Part of broader AI-native transformation strategy.

**Key Challenge:** Balancing urgent board commitment timeline with meaningful skill development and cultural change.

---

## What Is Jarvis?

Internal AI development platform (confirmed choice after CJ's bake-off evaluation process). Provides AI-augmented development capabilities for GHX engineering teams.

## Goals

### Strategic (Board Level)
- 60% AI productivity improvement (CJ's commitment)
- Demonstrate GHX is leaning into AI strategically
- Create optionality (cost savings OR reinvestment capacity)

### Operational (Engineering)
- Improve throughput and cycle time
- Reduce toil and manual work
- Enable teams to ship faster with AI augmentation

### Individual (Career)
- Develop skills that are industry table stakes
- Stay competitive in job market
- Make daily work easier/faster/better

---

## Current Issues

### Messaging Problems ⚠️
- "Not a performance review" contradicted by evaluation rubrics, action plans, development plans
- Timeline feels rushed/arbitrary without context
- Anxiety about consequences for low assessments
- HR delivery reinforces performance management optics

### Recommended Fixes
- Reframe as support/skill development opportunity
- Be transparent about board commitment context
- Remove performance management language
- Clarify August 15 is reporting milestone, not individual deadline
