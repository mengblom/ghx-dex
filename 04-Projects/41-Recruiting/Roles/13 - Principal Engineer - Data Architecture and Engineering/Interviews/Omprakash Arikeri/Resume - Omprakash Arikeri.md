**Bottom line:** Resume creates impression of data architecture depth that doesn't exist. Interview revealed limited database diversity (4 in 23 years), no data warehouse work, no advanced data modeling, no migration leadership, and communication challenges that would prevent effective enablement of application teams. The resume overstates data architecture specialization - candidate is actually a generalist backend developer with management trajectory, not the hands-on data architecture specialist this role requires.
