## Summary

Designed a modular metadata-driven data model and classification Pyspark framework that with master data management and governance, applies automated data classification and cleansing rules while ingesting data into a centralized medallion style data lake for Fabric/OneLake, SAP, Snowflake and Salesforce consumption.

The data arrives in the data lake as trusted and cataloged using Unity Catalog and made available for any operational, analytical or AI training models with full confidence and compliance to all regulatory and corporate requirements via OneLake.
