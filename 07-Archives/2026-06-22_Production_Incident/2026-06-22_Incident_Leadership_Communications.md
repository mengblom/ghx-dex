## Communication Strategy Analysis

### Timing
- **19:38 MT** - Executive summary after ~17 hours of incident work, allowing facts to stabilize
- **23:52 MT** - Formal handoff after ~21 hours, at appropriate timezone transition point

### Audience Adaptation
- **To VP/Director (Curtis/CJ):** Business impact focus, quantified metrics, systemic issues, accountability
- **To Incident Team:** Operational clarity, authority designation, priority guidance

### Key Messaging Principles
1. **Transparency:** Called out DevOps knowledge gaps and vendor dependencies explicitly
2. **Accountability:** Acknowledged process gaps requiring RCA
3. **Customer Focus:** Led with customer impact (orders, documents, major accounts)
4. **Action-Oriented:** Clear next steps with timeline expectations
5. **Authority Clarity:** Explicit handoff of decision-making authority

### What Was Not Done (Noteworthy)
- No minimizing or sugarcoating of issues
- No blame directed at individuals (systemic framing)
- No over-promising on timeline (realistic "goal" language)
- No abandonment (formal handoff vs. disappearing)
