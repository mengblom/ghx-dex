## Technical Root Cause Detail

### Monolithic Coupling Issue

**Problem Statement:** Hotfix intended to address single remaining issue (email-to-PDF conversion failures) could not be deployed in isolation due to:
1. Monolithic deployment footprint
2. Tight coupling between Exchange components
3. Build/release processes that bundle unrelated changes

**Collateral Damage:** eInvoicing modification included in hotfix package disrupted AS2 (Applicability Statement 2) flows - a B2B protocol for EDI communications.

**Architectural Implication:** Inability to deploy targeted fixes without testing/releasing unrelated code increases incident risk and extends resolution time.

### AS2 Flow Impact

**What is AS2:** Secure, reliable B2B protocol for EDI transmission (purchase orders, invoices, etc.)  
**Customer Impact:** Orders/documents failed to transmit between trading partners  
**Duration:** ~2.5 hours (EU), ~2.3 hours (US)  
**Mitigation:** Rollback to June 23 stable state
