- Proposal from Radha:
	- POC Approach
		- Rebuild document classification and extraction with a Claude / LLM based solution
		- But keep UIPath for Business Rules 
		- Drawbacks
			- Does not solve the problem of clunky business rules in UIPath
				- Background:
					- A couple of years ago, early version of GFax was very restrictive, frustrated a lot of customers - $10M business was at risk
					- Made significant 
	- Short term proposal
		- Claude for classification
		- Use BAutomate for Human in The Loop (HiTL)
- My feedback
	- We should find an approach that
		1. Gives us an incremental approach towards the end state solution (LLM based classification and extraction, purpose built business rules engine)
		2. Does not require us to temporarily integrate BAutomate (since the intention would be to sunset that a short time later)
- Decision:
	- We should recommend a purpose built approach that:
		1.  
		2. Allows us to start immediately on the most difficult/time consuming part - purpose built business rules engine
