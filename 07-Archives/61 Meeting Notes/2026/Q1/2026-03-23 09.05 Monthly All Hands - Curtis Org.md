## January YTD 2026 Financial Performance

**New Bookings**
- Achieved: YTD North America bookings of $5.7M - strong start despite January being typically the lightest booking month
- Performance partially supported by 2025 carryover deals closing after year-end
- Focus: Continue momentum in new customer signings (most forward-looking indicator)
- Outlook: Bookings signal healthy demand and position well for revenue conversion ahead

**CARR (Contracted Annual Recurring Revenue)**
- Achieved: Core North America CARR (excluding Inventory Management, Legacy & Explorer) ended January at $353.6M, growing at 12.3% annualized rate
- Focus: Driving sustained double-digit growth through selling multi-year recurring subscriptions while maintaining high customer satisfaction to minimize churn
- Outlook: CARR performance remains on track
- Note: CARR reflects predictable revenue locked in, giving GHX stability to invest in products, customers, and future

**Revenue**
- Achieved: YTD revenue is $39.1M, up $4.1M (+11.7% YoY) vs. prior year
- Focus: Every dollar counts - focus on minimizing customer churn and getting in front of anything we see at risk
- Outlook: As we finalize 2026 targets, 13.5% YoY subscription growth supported by strong billings, renewals, and new bookings positions us well heading into the new year

**EBITDA (Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization)**
- Achieved: YTD delivered $13.8M in Mgmt EBITDA, a 4.1% margin expansion from last year despite modest cost growth from higher headcount & hosting costs
- Focus: Maintain margin discipline by managing costs, controlling hosting and cybersecurity spend and sustaining strong revenue performance
- Outlook: We expect continued EBITDA strength driven by operating discipline and revenue momentum, while maintaining flexibility to invest in strategic growth initiatives

**Key Definitions**:
- CARR = Contracted Annual Recurring Revenue
- EBITDA = Earnings before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization
- COGS = Cost of Goods Sold are direct costs to produce or deliver GHX services
- YTD = Year to Date
- YoY = Year over Year

## AI Guidance

- AI can do a lot, but needs to be constrained
- Skills are how we define a process that constrains how it works
- 5 skills I've been working with:
  - **Grill me** – to align on expectation
  - **PRD Generation** – to document what the product/feature should be
  - **PRD to issues** – to convert the feature list into stories to work on
  - **TDD** – test driven development to validate
  - **Architecture review** – to validate architecture is organized for agents
