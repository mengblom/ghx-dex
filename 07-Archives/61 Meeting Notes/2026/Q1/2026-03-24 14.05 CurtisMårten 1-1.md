### Discussion about the homegrown LLM based solution to replace UiPath, as alternative to BAutomate ###

- Strong alignment from leadership team on moving forward
    - No pushback on proposed direction
    - Ramesh, Rada, and Greg Thompson all expressed support
    - All three thanked for “flying air cover” during presentation
- Timeline concerns raised
    - Ramesh suggested 3-6 months (possibly up to 12 months) implementation
    - Pushback on extended timeline - needs to be much faster
    - Business rules identified as primary bottleneck, not core AI solution
- Business rules complexity
    - Address parsing challenges (different customer formats, attention lines, department names)
    - Current manual workarounds could be solved by cloud-based solutions
    - Recommendation to focus on core solution first, address business rules later
- Happiest Minds Vendor
	- Happiest Minds reached out proposing AI/ML expertise for project
	- Two potential use cases discussed:
	    1. Building core solution (not recommended)
	    2. Manual processing backup for orders where AI isn’t 100% confident
	- Strong preference to keep core development in-house
	    - Build internal AI capabilities rather than outsource learning
	    - Use contractors for other tasks to free up best engineers for AI project
	- Next steps: Ramesh requesting formal proposal from Happiest Minds
	    - Will scrutinize team expertise and experience
	    - High bar for proving value over internal development
