- Discussion with Curtis on OKRs
	- Pushback from me on *Our grand boss has an idea that “if there is a deliverable, it will stay top of mind”* (from Eric on the topic of weekly AI reports from each manager)
		- I resent the idea that we are asking people to sink time into busy work, with no intention of consuming the material produced, only to "keep the pressure on".
		- People are not stupid... they are capable of doing the math: how many managers do we have, is CJ (or anyone else) really going to read all these reports produced? Couple that with the stress from real work, and people will get resentful really quickly.
