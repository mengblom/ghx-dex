- Proposed comms from Tasha:
	- *Candidates above the 75th percentile will not be auto-rejected. Recruiters will use their judgment to advance or decline these candidates, flagging exceptions for review where appropriate.*
	- *Counteroffers will be reviewed, not automatically declined. Each situation will be assessed case-by-case through the offer approval team.*
	- *For manager, senior, hard-to-fill, or long-tenured-open roles, recruiters will actively source between the midpoint and 75th percentile — including high-quality candidates who may require flexibility.*
	- *For standard roles, midpoint is the target — not a floor. We should not default to above-midpoint compensation for entry or mid-level positions.*
	- *Any offer above the 75th percentile requires CTO approval before extension.*
