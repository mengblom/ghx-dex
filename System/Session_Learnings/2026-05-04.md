## [14:40] - Planning Philosophy: Quarter-Ahead Detail is Waste

**What happened:** During 1x1 with [[Laura_Taylor]], discussed detailed planning timelines. Articulated position that story/epic-level planning a whole quarter in advance creates waste since those plans will inevitably change.

**Why it matters:** This is a core leadership philosophy that will come up repeatedly in prioritization and process discussions. Clear articulation helps maintain consistency in decision-making.

**Principle:** Plan at the right fidelity for the time horizon. Detailed breakdown far in advance optimizes for a stable world that doesn't exist.
